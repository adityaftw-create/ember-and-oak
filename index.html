The Space

A room that invites you to linger

Designed around the warmth of the open kitchen, our dining room balances the rustic honesty of oak and stone with considered modern lighting and intimate seating.

Whether it's a quiet evening for two or a long celebratory dinner, the space shapes itself around the occasion.

  • Private dining room for up to 14 guests
  • Chef's counter — 6 seats, live fire view
  • Curated cocktail and natural wine programme
  • Seasonal tasting menus on request
